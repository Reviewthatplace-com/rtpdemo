About Us

My name is Dr. Osama Ansari, Board Certified by the American Board of Internal Medicine, and I would firstly like to welcome you to West Orlando Internal Medicine.

I practice here after having graduated with the highest honors from the American University of Antigua and completing my Internal Medicine residency at Methodist Dallas Medical Center in 2014.

I came back home to Orlando, Florida in order to join the highly experienced medical team at Florida Hospital to make sure I could help serve my community.
Over the past ten years, I have been actively involved in primary care and hospital medicine, as well as numerous community health and wellness initiatives.

Additionally, I currently serve as a member of the Glycemic Management Team and as the Unit Medical Director at Florida Hospital. I have been recognized for my diligent patient care across several platforms.
