About Dr. Priti Modi

Dr. Priti Modi is a graduate of the UC Davis School of Medicine, Diplomate of American Board of Internal Medicine, Fellow of American College of Physicians, Master of American Board of Diabetes , Fellow of American Board of Geriatrics.

Dr. Priti Modi has provided medical care at her private practice since 1995. She believes that by listening attentively to her patients, she is able to obtain a thorough assessment of their physical and mental conditions. Combined with her many years of experience, and her sincere desire to help those who seek her care, allow her the ability to truly practice the “Art and Science of Healing”
