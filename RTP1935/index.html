About Us

Medical Heights Medical Center was founded in 1984 under the name of Southwest Clinic, with the passage of the years it would become what it is today. During 31 years, the practice has offered diligently and successfully the services of Gynecology, Family Care, Pediatrics, Surgery, Endoscopy, and Cardiology. Our goal is to be loyal to the community providing excellent care. Here in Medical Heights we are a family and each and every patient who crosses our door becomes part of this family too. Our efforts are directed to treating patients with respect, love, and dignity.

Our Services

FAMILY PRACTICE

Our family physicians are devoted to comprehensive health care for people of all ages. Our primary care provides continuing and comprehensive health care for the individual and family across all ages, genders, diseases, and parts of the body.

OB/GYN

Our OB/GYN physician is an expert at the care of female reproductive organs’ health and at the management of obstetric complications, even through surgery. Dr. Win provide medical and surgical care to women and have particular expertise in pregnancy, childbirth, and disorders of the reproductive system.

PEDIATRICS

Our Pediatrician focuses on the physical, emotional, and social health of infants, children, adolescents, and young adults from birth to 21 years. She is developmentally oriented and trained in skilled assessment; her patient-care lens is focused on prevention, detection, and management of physical, behavioral, developmental, and social problems that affect children.

MINOR SURGERIES, DOT & IMMIGRATIONAL PHYSICALS

At Medical Heights, we offer minor surgeries for treatments that involve cutting or stitching tissue.In addition, Dr. Kyi is now certified to perform DOT physicals. This physical is designed to test your blood pressure, vision, and hearing to ensure you have the capacity to safely and responsibly perform DOT duties.
