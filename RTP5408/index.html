About Us

Americare Wellness shares and embraces a common goal, which is to serve its patients with unconditional values of professionalism, respect, integrity, dignity, and empowerment, while understanding that aging is a natural process of human life. Together, these values create an internal understanding known as – PRIDE

P – Professionalism

At AW all employees are trained to high standards and expected to work under a strict code of ethics, honor, and good judgment.

R – Respect

At AW all employees shall embrace the true understanding of what it means to have respect for others, regardless of race, gender, age, and cultural background. AW encourages and guides employees to behave with high standards of respect for all its stakeholders.

I – Integrity

At AW all employees are expected to operate with high standards of honesty. AW encourages truthfulness behavior, with zero tolerance for unethical manners.

D – Dignity

AW honors its patients’ rights and preserves their privacy.

E – Empowerment

At AW all employees are empowered to seek the best possible outcomes and make the patients’ needs their first priority.

Our Services

Primary Care

Selecting a primary care physician is an important first in managing your health, and at Americare Wellness, we are there to hold your hand on your journey to living a healthier life.

Preventive Care

Preventive Care Offerings Complete Physicals, Blood Pressure, Diabetes, and Cholesterol Tests, Routine Vaccinations, PSA Screening and EKG.

Procedures

No matter your medical needs, it is always best to work with a partner you can trust. At Americare Wellness, we provide a comprehensive offering of services to suit a broad spectrum of healthcare needs.

Wellness Care

These days people are looking for healthcare solutions that will not only prevent aspects of negative health, but additionally, they are interested in achieving a state of positive health or a state of more complete well-being.

Long Term Care

These services help people live as independently and safely as possible when they can no longer perform everyday activities on their own.

Weight Loss

One of safest ways to lose weight is under the supervision of a qualified physician that is specially trained in medical weight loss.

Telemedicine

Telemedicine refers to a range of means and methods in the practice of caring for patients remotely when the provider and patient are not physically present with each other.

House Calls

House calls are often appropriate for patients who are homebound with an immediate or acute need for care.
