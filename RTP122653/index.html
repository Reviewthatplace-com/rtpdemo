Why To Choose Us?

Powers Pediatrics has been serving the community since 2008. We are in our brand new facility on Rose Ave & Orange Blossom Trail since November 2013.

Expert Services

All of our staff is highly experienced working with children, assuring only the best treatment and information for the children that visit us.

Same Day Appointment

Our wait times are generally always less than 15 minutes with an appointment and same day appointments are available if need be.

24 Hours Services

We also have a 24 hours patient support service which connects you straight to our doctor for after hour’s questions or emergencies.
