About Us

At Oncology & Hematology of Oakland, your hope can be restored by an expert team of cancer specialists and some of the most advanced, capable and compassionate cancer care in the region. Right here in Oakland since 1988.

Oncology & Hematology of Oakland(OHO) in Rochester, MI serves patients in the communities of Oakland, Macomb and Lapeer counties. Dr. Mukesh Shah and his team provides the emotional, educational and psychological support that patients and their families need when battling cancer. Along with one-on-one personal care they offer in-office chemotherapy, and referrals for radiation therapy and surgery. Dr. Shah also treats patients with blood disorders ranging from leukemias, anemias, bleeding disorders, excessive clotting of the blood and others.

Our Services

Patient services

Our entire staff is here to support and encourage you on your journey back to wellness. We understand you, your family and loved ones will have many feelings – denial, anger, fear, sadness, loneliness – but remember hope, determination, confidence, and courage are also feelings that can help you take control and fight your disease.

Complete Hemotology services

In addition to tests and medical oncology therapies, Dr. Shah works closely with other cancer specialists, including radiation oncologists, surgeons and other physicians. Dr. Shah also provides an array of oncology.

Compassionate, patient-centered care

The OHO team is dedicated to delivering leading-edge cancer care close to home. They stay abreast of the latest, most effective methods for treating cancer aggressively, helping you stay out of the hospital – and get on with your life after.

Cancer Diagnosis & Staging

Diagnosis is the first step toward curing cancer. Detecting cancer, identifying how it affects the body and classifying its type and stage of development are vital to developing a treatment plan that is effective and appropriate for each individual.
