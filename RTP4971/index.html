About us

At ChiroHealth Chiropractic Sports and Rehabilitation, we offer care to individuals through conservative means. We understand the importance of your wellness. To achieve your objectives, you have come to expect the highest levels of service and patient care. As a result, we continuously commit ourselves to meeting and exceeding your expectations. To us, providing a total health care experience means dedicated and friendly staff, flexible and convenient hours and the highest quality care available. At ChiroHealth, we have one mission: to help patients eliminate or reduce pain, and get back to enjoying their life and activities as quickly as possible. Our doctors provide the latest and most advanced treatments in the Pearland and surrounding areas. Backed by training and education in elite sports medicine programs, ChiroHealth has unique and targeted protocols for each type of patient and every type of injury. We work in conjunction with specialized physicians of multiple disciplines to ensure that your treatment allows you to obtain optimal health. Whether you are a professional or amateur athlete, weekend warrior, Starting your first training program, suffering from work-related, repetitive stress injuries, a mother-to-be, or just suffering from the daily grind, ChiroHealth will design a regimen specifically for your needs.
